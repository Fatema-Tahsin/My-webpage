My Country

Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a vibrant South Asian country known for its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and resilient people. It is shaped by rivers that nourish fertile land and support agriculture, one of the nation’s key economic pillars. The country blends tradition with rapid urban growth, especially in Dhaka, its energetic capital. Famous for the Sundarbans and Cox’s Bazar, Bangladesh offers remarkable natural beauty. Its history reflects a strong spirit of independence and identity, celebrated through language, festivals, and arts.

Today, Bangladesh continues progressing in education, industry, and technology while preserving its unique heritage. Its future remains bright with ongoing development ahead.

My City

My birth city is Chittagong. It is Bangladesh’s major port city, tucked between hills and the Bay of Bengal. It mixes a busy maritime economy with relaxing coastal views, making it a place where industry and nature live side by side.

What makes Chittagong special

  1. The Sea & the Port: Home to Bangladesh’s largest seaport—ships, docks, and maritime trade shape everyday life.
  2. Hills & Greenery: Even as a big city, it has rolling green hills around many neighborhoods.
  3. Beaches: Patenga and Parki Beach are go-to spots for sunsets and fresh sea breeze.
  4. Food: Mezban beef, shutki, and coastal seafood give the city its signature flavors.
